Hello World

Hanyun Wang is currently an associate professor with the School of Surveying and Mapping, Information Engineering University, China. He received his PhD degree from National University of Defense Technology in 2015. He was a visiting PhD student at Xiamen University from 2011 to 2014. He has authored over 50 articles in journals and conferences, such as IEEE TPAMI, ACM Computing Surveys(CSUR), ISPRS Journal P&RS, IEEE TGRS, IEEE TITS, and IEEE CVPR. His research interests focus on point clouds and remote sensing images processing and understanding. He served as guest editor on the journals including JAG and IET Computer Vision, and organization committee member of international conference sessions including ICPR and ICVR. He also served as reviewer for many journals, such as IEEE TIP, IEEE TGRS, IEEE TITS, ISPRS Journal P&RS, and JAG.

Email: why.scholar@gmail.com

Research Interest

His general research interest is to develop learning based method for computer vision tasks. Recently, His research interests include:

  • 3D Computer Vision
  • Deep Learning
  • Point Cloud Processing and Multimodal Scene Understanding
  • Remote Sensing Images Processing and Understanding
